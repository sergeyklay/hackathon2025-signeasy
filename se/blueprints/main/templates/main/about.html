About

SignEasy is a document signing and analysis platform that helps individuals and businesses manage agreements, streamline signatures, and gain valuable insights. Our platform is designed to simplify complex processes, save time, and reduce errors.

Send Document Workflow

The sender flow in SignEasy is a simple and straightforward process that allows users to upload a document, analyze it, and send it for signing. The sender flow is designed to be intuitive and user-friendly, making it easy for users to prepare documents for signing and send them to recipients.
